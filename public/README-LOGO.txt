PUT YOUR LOGO HERE.

1. Name your logo file exactly:  logo.png
2. Place it in this folder (the "public" folder).
3. That's it — it appears automatically on the login page.

Notes:
- PNG with a transparent background looks best (the login panel is dark).
- If you must use a different format, you can use logo.svg or logo.jpg,
  but then change the filename in src/pages/Login.jsx (search for "/logo.png").
- If no logo.png is present, the app simply shows the text title and no broken image.
